New York City and Washington Vacation

12/30/2021

On the 21st of December, we woke up at four in the morning. It was a Tuesday. We ate breakfast and left the house at five. It was a cold morning so I wore a jacket. It was very dark outside, so it was hard to see what was around. We got onto the car and Uncle Khai Yang started driving us to the airport. I got to hear many songs on the radio when we were on the car. We parked our car in theParkingSpot's parking lot. We later then got transported to the airport. We got on a flight to New York City. The flight was approximately three hours long. Unfortunately, I got a headache during the flight. We hopped on an Uber and headed for out hotel. We later then started walking around New York City. We reached Times Square after quite some time and my legs were going to fall. That night, we ate some ramen that night, but I did not have my appetite. I felt better that night as I enjoyed the night and ate my (late) dinner. DAY TWO - 22nd December 2021
